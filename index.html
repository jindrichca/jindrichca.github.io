Hi, I'm Charlie


I am an electrical engineering student at The Collge of Charleston.

This site has been created as a way to explore subjects that I am interested in and to organize what I have learned in such a way that I can easily go back over it at any time. While the site is specifically geared toward my interests and learning style, my hope is that others may find these resources useful too.

The site will mainly focus on electrical engineering and computer science topics, but it may include any topic that I am interested enough in to write a post about.

Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy!
